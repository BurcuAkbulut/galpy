galpy.util.bovy_conversion.force_in_pcMyr2(vo, ro)¶
NAME:
force_in_pcMyr2
PURPOSE:
convert a force or acceleration to pc/Myr^2
INPUT:
vo - velocity unit in km/s ro - length unit in kpc
OUTPUT:
conversion from units where vo=1. at ro=1.
