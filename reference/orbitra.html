galpy.orbit.Orbit.ra¶

Orbit.ra(*args, **kwargs)¶

NAME:

ra

PURPOSE:

return the right ascension

INPUT:

t - (optional) time at which to get ra

obs=[X,Y,Z] - (optional) position of observer (in kpc) (default=[8.0,0.,0.]) OR Orbit object that corresponds to the orbit of the observer

ro= distance in kpc corresponding to R=1. (default: 8.0)

OUTPUT:

ra(t)

HISTORY:

2011-02-23 - Written - Bovy (NYU)
