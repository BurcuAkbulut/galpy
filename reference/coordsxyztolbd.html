galpy.util.bovy_coords.XYZ_to_lbd¶

galpy.util.bovy_coords.XYZ_to_lbd(X, Y, Z, degree=False)¶
NAME:
XYZ_to_lbd
PURPOSE:
transform from rectangular Galactic coordinates to spherical Galactic coordinates works with vector inputs
INPUT:
X - component towards the Galactic Center (in kpc; though this obviously does not matter)) Y - component in the direction of Galactic rotation (in kpc) Z - component towards the North Galactic Pole (kpc) degree - (Bool) if True, return l and b in degrees
OUTPUT:
[l,b,d] in (rad,rad,kpc) For vector inputs [:,3]
HISTORY:
2009-10-24 - Written - Bovy (NYU)
